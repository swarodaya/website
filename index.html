Residential Course

SwarYog, the ancient Indian science of breath awareness has been practiced since ages by yogis of India to make their lives more meaningful and fulfilling. SwarYog helps you in a way that rarely any other science can accomplish. It helps you to know yourself on various levels and with that knowledge helps you to create abundance at all levels.

The Yoga Sutras of Patañjali are ancient Indian text consisting of 196 sutras, divided into four chapters. The Yoga Sutras were compiled prior to 400 BC by Sage Patanjali, summarizing yoga from older traditions. The Yog Sutras present, in a condensed form, the vast essence of life and methodically shares the ways of purposful living. The heart of these teachings is the eightfold path of yoga - also known as the Ashtanga Yog. These are also called the eight limbs of Patanjali, and they describe the practical steps to follow in order to be in a state of eternal flow, known as Kaivalya.

Swarodaya - in it’s training programmes, brings to you the combined knowledge of these two sacred and deep subjects in the most lucid, easy to understand and practical manner that helps you to lead a fulfilling life filled with harmony and prosperity.

Vastu Shastra

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that is based upon the laws of nature and its effects on human dwellings. It formlates the rules for living spaces that would ensure harmony, prosperity, and health for its residents. These laws are based on the five elements of nature — Water, Air, Fire, Earth, and Space — and their effects on human life.

We at Swarodaya, provide simple and effective solutions to make your living space vastu compliant, and we do this without recommending structural demolitions. We study the floor plan and interior design of your residence, and then align it with your horoscope based on your planetary strengths, to give customized recommendations for non-destructive changes. These changes help you realize your goals and aspirations, and also help you resolve your life challenges and problems.

We help you to:

  • Gain new career opportunities and growth in your field of work.
  • Improve your finances
  • Strengthening of relations within family, friends, and colleagues.
  • Help children learn better and perform at the best of their abilities.
  • Reduce mental stress, enhance clarity of thought and increase the peace of mind.

Astrology

Jyotish - Astrology - is an ancient traditional Hindu system of astrology, and is one of the most time-tested and oldest systems in the world. The Sanskrit word ‘Jyotish’ (ja-yo-teesh) means ‘light of God.’ The Light that guides the inner self, reducing uncertainty, allowing us to plan our future.

At Swarodaya, we provide you with effective astrological solutions, which include use of gemstones and mantras to help you overcome obstacles in life with ease, and achieve your goals.

We analyze your birth-chart to help you understand:

  • Your inner self, your strengths and your weakness, your natural skills and abilities.
  • Understand the cycles of your life: your favorable and unfavorable years.
  • Provide astrological recommendations to overcome the difficult periods of life through gemstones and other solutions.
  • Provide guidance in matters of education, on the choice of careers, profession and other important aspects of life.
  • Help determine when to begin a project, a new enterprise or relocation.

Numerology and Signature Report

Numerology is the study of numbers related to your life. It is used to determine your personality, your nature, your strengths and your weaknesses, and your state of mind. At Swarodaya, we identify your favorable and unfavorable numbers, and guide you on their usage in attaining their maximum benefit in terms of progress.

We help you understand:

  • The significance of the numbers associated with your life and the qualities associated with them.
  • We help you understand your strengths and weaknesses based on those numbers.
  • Your favorable and unfavorable numbers. How to use this information while making important decisions in life as well as in day-to-day activities.
  • Recommendations to make changes in your signature, based on your numbers, to achieve maximum prosperity and happiness, and make your signature powerful and effective.

Complete Report

The complete report is comprehensive in nature and counsels you on three major domains of life: Vastu, Astro, and Numero-Signature. It includes:
