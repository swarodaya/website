Swar Yog

Swaryog is an ancient indian science of breath awareness that has been practiced by yogis, spiritual masters, and seers of India, since thousands of years, to create a life of prosperity and abundance.

Swar is breath and yog is to unite. Swaryog is a science where we unite with our breath to gain an understanding of the self and our universe. Swar or breath can be understood in terms of Nadis and Tatwas. When the breath flows from the left nostril, it is referred as the Chandra Swar and is associated with the Ida nadi of our body. When the breath flows from the right nostril, it is referred as the Surya Swar and is associated with the Pingala Nadi of our body. When the breath flows from the both the nostrils, at once, it is referred as the Shunya Swar and is connected with the Sushumna nadi of our body. The breath, depending on the flow is associated with the different nadis, which in turn, has different effects on our mind and body.

To add to the effect of these nadis, is the dimension of ‘tatwas’ or elements. The place in the nostril through which the breath flows, is referred as the ‘element’ or ‘tatwa’ of the breath. There are five different elements (Space, Air, Fire, Earth, and Water) that can flow in a nostril at a given time.

This combined play of Nadis and Tatwas is the science of Swaryog. It not only helps you raise your awareness and, connect deeply within, and feel grounded and rooted, but also is of immense help in decision making, event planning, foreseeing events, creating abundance and prosperity.

During the session, students learn to recognize the Nadis, the Tatwas and understand their properties and usage for their personal and professional use.

Patanjali Yog Sutra

The Yog Sutras were codified by Sage Patanjali about 5000 years ago in Sanskrit. The sutras, 196 in number and divided into 4 Padas or chapters, deals in depth with subjects like Samadhi and its forms, Sadhana and its various methods, Siddhis - their attainments and results, and Kaivalya - the science and art of being in Flow. The popular Ashtang Yog form the core of these Sutras. The aim of the Yog Sutras is to help an individual attain immense clarity about the self, life, people and situations, giving a framework and structure to daily living. It is a guide to raising potential to the maximum and living life to the fullest, maintaining calm and composure even in stressful situations. The Yog Sutras helps us identify, and then plug, our energy leakages on mental and physical level, helping us evolve, be efficient, better organized and gain inner strength.

During the course, Acharya Seema renders her understanding of these complex sutras in simple english and help the student grasp their crux and help make use of the understanding gained to simplify and enhance life.

Vastu Shastra

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that is based upon the laws of nature and its effects on human dwellings. It formlates the rules for living spaces that would ensure harmony, prosperity, and health for its residents. These laws are based on the five elements of nature — Water, Air, Fire, Earth, and Space — and their effects on human life.

We at Swarodaya, provide simple and effective solutions to make your living space vastu compliant, and we do this without recommending structural demolitions. We study the floor plan and interior design of your residence, and then align it with your horoscope based on your planetary strengths, to give customized recommendations for non-destructive changes. These changes help you realize your goals and aspirations, and also help you resolve your life challenges and problems.

We help you to:

  • Gain new career opportunities and growth in your field of work.
  • Improve your finances
  • Strengthening of relations within family, friends, and colleagues.
  • Help children learn better and perform at the best of their abilities.
  • Reduce mental stress, enhance clarity of thought and increase the peace of mind.

Astrology

Jyotish - Astrology - is an ancient traditional Hindu system of astrology, and is one of the most time-tested and oldest systems in the world. The Sanskrit word ‘Jyotish’ (ja-yo-teesh) means ‘light of God.’ The Light that guides the inner self, reducing uncertainty, allowing us to plan our future.

At Swarodaya, we provide you with effective astrological solutions, which include use of gemstones and mantras to help you overcome obstacles in life with ease, and achieve your goals.

We analyze your birth-chart to help you understand:

  • Your inner self, your strengths and your weakness, your natural skills and abilities.
  • Understand the cycles of your life: your favorable and unfavorable years.
  • Provide astrological recommendations to overcome the difficult periods of life through gemstones and other solutions.
  • Provide guidance in matters of education, on the choice of careers, profession and other important aspects of life.
  • Help determine when to begin a project, a new enterprise or relocation.
